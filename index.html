About Us

Balance Bharat Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating harmony between society, economy, and environment. We work at the grassroots level to empower communities, support education, promote health awareness, and encourage sustainable development across India.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To build a balanced Bharat where every individual has equal opportunities, dignity, and access to education, health, and sustainable livelihoods.

Mission

To empower communities through education, awareness programs, skill development, and social initiatives that promote long-term positive change.

Values

Integrity, inclusiveness, sustainability, transparency, and compassion guide every action we take.

Our Programs

Education & Skill Development

Supporting underprivileged students with quality education, digital literacy, and skill-based training for employment.

Health & Wellness

Organizing health camps, mental wellness sessions, and awareness programs to improve community health.

Environment & Sustainability

Promoting tree plantation drives, waste management awareness, and sustainable living practices.

Community Outreach

Working directly with communities through workshops, campaigns, and social initiatives.
